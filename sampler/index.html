What to search?

First, you need to click on "Login" in the upper right corner

and allow access to audio


In the search bar that appears: in the first field, enter what to look for, second - how much to show (default: 5 tracks)


You can also load tracks from a computer (wav or mp3) - click on: choose file

Click on the downloaded track - wait until fully loaded!


If not loaded or loaded on half - click on another, before load fully


On letter-keys, samples binds automatically

To bind samples on number-keys, press: SHIFT+number while playing the track


Checkbox "Stop after keyUp?" turns on a stopping track after key is released


SPACE play or pause the track


ENTER load random track from playlist


PRESS the buttons and ENJOY!!!
